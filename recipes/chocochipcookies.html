Chocolate Chip Cookies



Description

.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add this to the batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop dough in large spoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the oven for 10 min, or until edges are lightly browned.
