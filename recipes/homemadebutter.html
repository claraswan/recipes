Homemade Butter



Description

After I perfected my muffin recipe, I decided that the best complement to that would be some homemade butter. It's simple, fun, and probably burns a few calories.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh heavy whipping cream
  • pinch of salt
  • some cheese cloth

Steps

  1. Pour cream into a large mason jar, ensuring the cream doesn't take up more than a third of the jar.
  2. Add the salt to the jar and seal it.
  3. Vigorously shake the jar for roughly 5-10 minutes until the fat separates.
  4. Pour jar contents into some cheese cloth to drain the buttermilk from the lump of butter.
  5. Rinse the lump of butter with cold water and put it in a jar or butter dish. Enjoy!
