Homemade Butter



Description


Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh heavy whipping cream
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Whisk together dry ingredients
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients with the lemon zest.
  4. Toss blueberries in a couple spoonfuls of flour mixture and set aside.
  5. Make a small well in the dry mix and pour in the wet mix. Mix until JUST combined. Some lumps are fine.
  6. Fold in the blueberries.
  7. Fill muffin cups and top with a sprinkle of coarse sugar.
  8. Bake in the oven for 15-20 min, or until a skewer inserted comes out clean.
